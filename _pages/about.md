### **Yifeng** Xu (徐逸峰)

I am a first-year Master student in the [VIPL](http://vipl.ict.ac.cn/en) research group at [ICT](http://www.ict.cas.cn/), [CAS](https://english.cas.cn/). I am supervised by Prof. [Shiguang Shan](https://vipl.ict.ac.cn/people/~sgshan) and have close collaboration with Prof. [Zhenliang He](https://lynnho.github.io/). I received my B.Eng. from [HITsz](https://www.hitsz.edu.cn/index.html) in 2023, where I was fortunate to receive guidance from Prof. [Wenjie Pei](https://wenjiepei.github.io/). My research interests lie in generative models and low-level vision.
